    THIS WEEK'S CNN HERO 

    Unique program helps women escape streets, transform their lives

    Since 1997, Becca Stevens has dedicated her life to helping women escape prostitution, addiction and trafficking -- and providing a place for them to heal.

    CNN Hero Becca Stevens

    Becca Stevens and her Nashville-based nonprofit, Thistle Farms, have helped more than 200 women escape the streets and transform their lives.

    "They loved me until I could love myself."

    A woman from CNN Hero Becca Stevens' program in Nashville shows how she transformed her life from walking the streets to becoming a sales executive.

    Get Involved: Thistle Farms

    CNN Hero Craig Dodson

    Craig Dodson started a nonprofit cycling program for at-risk youth living in Richmond, Virginia, public housing. But he is much more than a coach to them.

    Riding through the pain

    A young bike racer shows how CNN Hero Craig Dodson's program helps him overcome the challenges of growing up in a Richmond, Virginia, housing project.

    CNN Hero Paula Claussen

    Since 1991, Paula Claussen and her group have crossed the Mexico border to build more than 1,250 free homes for impoverished families in Tijuana.

    Changing lives in one day

    CNN Hero Paula Claussen shows how her nonprofit, Project Mercy, can build a home in a single day for families living on the outskirts of Tijuana, Mexico.

    CNN Hero Harry Swimmer

    For 20 years, Harry Swimmer and his nonprofit have introduced hundreds of children with disabilities to the healing power of horses near Charlotte, North Carolina.

    Everyone's wild about Harry

    CNN Hero Harry Swimmer, 86, gives an inside look at a day on his horse farm, where his nonprofit, Mitey Riders, helps children with special needs.

    CNN Hero Luma Mufleh

    Since 2004, Luma Mufleh and her nonprofit, the Fugees Family, have provided a community of support for hundreds of refugee children in Clarkston, Georgia.

    From refugee to Fugees

    CNN Hero Luma Mufleh and young refugees from her program share the challenges of starting over in the United States -- and how soccer has helped.

    CNN Hero Shara Fisler

    Shara Fisler's nonprofit, the Ocean Discovery Institute, helps thousands of kids from one of San Diego's poorest communities discover science.

    CNN Hero Marquis Taylor

    Basketball helped Marquis Taylor stay on the right path. Today, he and his group use the sport, and an unusual method, to help young people become leaders.

    Passing the ball

    CNN Hero Marquis Taylor shares how his childhood experiences inspired him to help young people see their potential -- on and off the basketball court.

    CNN Hero Brandon Chrostowski

    Brandon Chrostowski is giving second chances to ex-offenders through his nonprofit restaurant and culinary training program in Cleveland.

    Life after Edwins

    Graduates of CNN Hero Brandon Chrostowski's program for ex-offenders show how they're now building successful culinary careers in Cleveland.

    CNN Hero Veronika Scott

    In 2010, Veronika Scott designed a convertible coat for the homeless. Today, she and her Detroit nonprofit hire the homeless to help manufacture it.

    The Empowerment Plan

    CNN Hero Veronika Scott shows how her nonprofit, the Empowerment Plan, creates jobs -- and dignity -- for Detroit's homeless.

    CNN Hero Sheldon Smith

    Sheldon Smith's Chicago nonprofit gives young fathers like himself the skills to be positive role models and responsible parents.

    Manning Up

    Sheldon Smith and his group, the Dovetail Project, have helped more than 200 young fathers in Chicago be positive role models and responsible parents.
