Ryan Rohm

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ryan Rohm is a string theorist at the University of North Carolina. He is one of the so-called "Princeton string quartet",[1] and is responsible for the development of heterotic string theory along with David Gross, Jeffrey A. Harvey and Emil Martinec,[1][2] the other members of the Princeton String Quartet.
